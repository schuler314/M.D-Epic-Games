HOW TO PLAY

Goal: Ride as far as possible without crashing. Earn points for distance and flips!

Player 1

  • Gas: [Up] or [Space]
  • Lean Back: [Left]
  • Lean Fwd: [Right]

Player 2

  • Gas: [W]
  • Lean Back: [A]
  • Lean Fwd: [D]

Player 1 (Pad 1)

  • Gas: [A] or [RT]
  • Lean Back: [D-Pad Left]
  • Lean Fwd: [D-Pad Right]

Player 2 (Pad 2)

  • Requires a 2nd controller. Uses the same bindings.

⚠️ Turn Device Sideways

Play in landscape mode for a wider view.

Mobile Controls

  • Use the translucent on-screen buttons!
  • ⚡ = Gas / Accelerate
  • ↺ = Lean Back (Wheelie/Backflip)
  • ↻ = Lean Forward (Frontflip)

Powerups

  • ⚡ Arrows Speed: Double engine power!
  • 🛡️ Crystal Shield: Bounces you back up if you crash.
  • ⭐ Gold Coin Coin: Bonus +1000 points.
  • 🎈 Balloon Float: Lower gravity for huge jumps.
