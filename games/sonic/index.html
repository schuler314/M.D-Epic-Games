HOW TO PLAY

CONTROLS

P1: Arrow Keys to Move & Jump
P2: W,A,S,D to Move & Jump

LIVES & LOOPS

You have 3 lives. Run fast (hold forward) into Loop-the-Loops to trigger the spiral. If you walk into them slowly, you'll just bump over the ramp safely.

POWERUPS

You can BANK up to 3 powerups! Press ENTER/SHIFT (P1) or E (P2) to use the oldest one.
