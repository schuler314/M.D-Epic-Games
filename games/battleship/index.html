Commander's Manual

Objective: Destroy the enemy's entire fleet before they destroy yours.

Gameplay: Click on the enemy grid to launch a missile. Red pegs indicate a HIT. White pegs indicate a MISS. Completely hit a ship to sink it.

1 Player Mode: Face an increasingly difficult AI across infinite levels. Winning a level grants you points and random powerups.

2 Player Mode: Pass and play on the same device. The screen will hide the board during transitions.

Powerups

  • Airstrike: Bombs an entire random row automatically.
  • Sonar: Click a target to safely reveal a 3x3 area without using a turn.
  • Double Tap: Grants an immediate extra shot this turn.
